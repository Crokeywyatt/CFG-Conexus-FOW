FUTURE OF WORK 2022

CELEBRATING FLEXIBLE WORK AND SHARING OUR COLLECTIVE KNOWLEDGE TO NAVIGATE
THE ROAD AHEAD IN THIS EVER-CHANGING WORLD

Key Themes

Join our Executive sponsors as they kick off the Conexus Global Future of Work event to hear their thoughts on the hot topics in the Future of Work conversation. The panel will reflect on the journey we have been on in recent years, the evolution of hybrid work, critical future skills and much more.

Collaboration

Skills that help us work together towards a common goal, allow us to share knowledge and combine abilities. Collaboration and communication are also two of the attributes that make humans so unique and successful. However, these skills require work and refinement for us to apply them effectively

Future Skills

It is more critical than ever that we understand the skills that deliver the greatest value today and tomorrow. Learn about the critical transferable skills required to navigate the constantly evolving landscape of our working environment and gain insights into the areas of professional development to focus on to stay relevant.

Networking

Hybrid work, if not managed well, can be hazardous to the health of employees' networks. We could simply accept this as a cost of hybrid working life. Or, as individuals, we can take actions to counteract these forces.So, what can you do to boost your network, when working remotely or back in the office?
