About Us

What We Do

Conexus is the Employee Resource Group that champions a flexible work community by creating a collaborative work environment, which enables team members to grow and thrive. Conexus focuses on technologies, best practices and innovative ways of thinking and doing which integrates people regardless of where, when and how they work.
